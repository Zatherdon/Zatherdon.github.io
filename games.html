    ClayTown Horror

    About:

    Enter into the twisted Dollhouse and experience the terrors within. ClayTown Horror is a Single-player horror game with a focus on story, puzzles, and a unique art-style. Monsters and mayhem roam the halls of this home and, in this first half of ClayTown Horror, you will come face to face with many of them. Use all the tools at your disposal and fight back against the evils of this place. Oh, and if you can, try and find out for yourself the mystery of what has happened in this quiet hilltop house.
